Dear Colleagues,

We're excited to announce that our **job postings for Economists** at the FTC's Bureau of Economics are now live!

- GS-13 (typically new Ph.D.s & post-docs): [https://www.usajobs.gov/job/851497400](https://www.usajobs.gov/job/851497400)
- GS-14 (some professional experience): [https://www.usajobs.gov/job/851497300](https://www.usajobs.gov/job/851497300)
- GS-15 (significant antitrust and/or consumer protection experience): [https://www.usajobs.gov/job/851496000](https://www.usajobs.gov/job/851496000)

Please feel free to share these links on social media and forward this message to anyone in your network who may be interested. Note that **the postings will close on December 17** so interested applicants should act quickly.

Applicants will need to submit a 2-page resume (not an academic CV) that clearly describes their paid and unpaid work experience (e.g. conducting research). These experience statements are essential for verifying minimum qualifications. U.S. citizenship is required.

We're also be hosting a **Virtual Recruitment Event on Thursday, December 11 at 1pm EST** to provide more insight into the FTC and the application process. Please share the Zoom link below with anyone interested.
